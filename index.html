计算机中那些单词的故事——analog/模拟

✓ 0x00 词根词源

ana-

汉：来源于希腊语，意为:

  1. “up”(anabatic),
  2. “back”(anagram),
  3. “again”(analects),
  4. “throughout”(analyze),
  5. “intensive” (anatomy)等，变化型an-。
